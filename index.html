AI-searchable archive

Structured so both people and AI systems can understand the content.
Ask things like “Which Michigan festivals happen near Lake St. Clair?” or “What county fairs are in St. Clair County?”

Festival database structure

  • Identity: name, city, county, type
  • Time: month, season, founded
  • Location: latitude and longitude
  • Narrative: short summary, atmosphere
  • Research: missing archive materials, traditions, source gaps

Research contributor portal

We’re looking for local historians, journalism students, hobby researchers, and volunteers who can help uncover archival materials from historical societies, libraries, fair boards, and newspaper collections.

  • Your role: document photos, fair books, posters, parade programs, and local records that are not yet online.
  • Our role: organize, summarize, design, and publish the material into a consistent atlas entry.
  • Credit: contributor names can later be listed on the pages they help enrich.
